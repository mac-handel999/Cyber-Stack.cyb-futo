About Cyber Stack


Welcome to Cyber Stack, your comprehensive online tech resource hub serving the Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO) community and the wider tech world. We've built this platform to be your go-to destination for navigating the vast and ever-evolving landscape of technology, with a special emphasis on Cybersecurity.


At Cyber Stack, we understand that staying current in the tech industry requires continuous learning and access to quality resources. That's why we've curated a diverse collection of materials to support your journey, whether you're just starting out or looking to advance your expertise. Our mission is to:

Educate: Provide accessible and comprehensive resources, workshops, and training sessions to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills to navigate the digital landscape securely.
Connect: Create a vibrant community where students, researchers, industry professionals, and anyone interested in cybersecurity can connect, collaborate, and learn from each other.
Innovate: Encourage exploration, research, and the development of innovative solutions to address emerging cybersecurity challenges.
Advocate: Raise awareness about the importance of cybersecurity best practices and promote a security-conscious mindset within FUTO and the wider community.
